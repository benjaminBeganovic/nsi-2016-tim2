Professors

How to add new CV Item ?

Go to "My CV" page and fill data, everything except Links is required, then press Add.

I added new CV Item but it does not show on my page, what now ?

After adding new CV Item, you can check "My Requests" page to see your pending requests. In order for CV Item to appear in your CV, Admin must approve it, after Admin approves your CV, you will be able to see it in "My CV".

I added CV Item, and it disappeared from My Reqests, but it also does not show in My CV.

This means that your CV Item was not approved. You can check though your CV data in "My History". "My History" page is designed for you to see all of your submited CV Items and their latest status, all you need to do is fill Date Range From and To, this would be dates when you suspect that you created CV Request.

Admins

What is difference between master and subcriteria ?

Master criteria is primary criteria from which all other criteria options are derived, there can be more than one Master criteria. For example, master criteria could be type of work : Science, Mathematics, Biology ... While subcriteria can be (derived from Biology) : Human Biology. Each subcriteria can be then derived further into more specific criteria.

What does "Professor History" page reprisent ?

This page is designed for Admins to view full history of all users of our application, in it you can search users and view their history of submited items, including those that have been deleted.

What are "New Requests" ?

New Requests is page in which Admin can Confirm or Reject CV Item submitted or Modified by professor, after reviewing the request you can choose one of two options : Confirm or Reject. After you Confirm or Reject an item, it will appear in Log page as latest change on that item.

What is Log and what are its options ?

Log stores data for review on some main changes made to CV Items. In Log you can also fix some mistakes made by Admin or CV Owners (Professors usually). Options shown in Review depends on latest CV Item status, and these are the options :
  • Confirm : In order to Confirm CV Item that was recently either Modified or Rejected.
  • Reject : In order to Reject CV Item that was recently either Modified or Confirmed.
  • Restore : In order to reactivate, or undelete CV Item that was recently Deleted. After restoring an item, it will show in New Requests as MODIFIED, while it will show in Log with its true Status RESTORED. This Item will need further Review, meaning it will need to be either Confirmed or Rejected.
